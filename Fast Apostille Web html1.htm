Frequently Asked Questions

What is an Apostille?
An Apostille is an official certificate issued by the Government to verify the signature of a public official on a document for use abroad.
How long does legalisation take?
Our Standard service takes 5 working days, while our Express service provides next-day legalisation for urgent requirements.
Do you accept original documents?
Yes, most documents require the original physical copy to be submitted, although some can be processed via digital notarised copies.
